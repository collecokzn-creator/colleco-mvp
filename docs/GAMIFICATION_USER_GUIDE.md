## â“ FAQ

### General

**Q: Is gamification free?**
A: Yes! 100% free for all users.

**Q: Do points expire?**
A: No, points never expire. Your account lasts forever!

**Q: Can I transfer points to someone else?**
A: Not currently, but we're exploring gifting options.

**Q: Can I convert points to cash?**
A: No, but discounts = real savings!

### Privacy

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Absolutely. We're POPI Act compliant with privacy-by-design principles.

**Q: Can I delete my data?**
A: Yes. Go to Settings > Privacy > Delete Account (30-day grace period).

**Q: Who sees my leaderboard rank?**
A: Only users who opt-in. Others see anonymized names.

**Q: Can I opt out after opting in?**
A: Yes, anytime via Privacy Settings.

### Points & Challenges

**Q: Why didn't I get points for a booking?**
A: Make sure:
- Booking is confirmed (not pending)
- You're logged in
- Integration is active (check with support if issue persists)

**Q: How do I complete a challenge faster?**
A: Focus on actions that count toward that challenge. Check the challenge description for details.

**Q: Can I see my point history?**
A: Yes! Go to Gamification > Achievements > View History

**Q: What happens when I complete a challenge?**
A: You get:
1. Point reward (auto-awarded)
2. Badge unlock (if eligible)
3. Notification
4. Progress toward next tier

### Streaks

**Q: What time do streaks reset?**
A: Midnight South African Standard Time (SAST/UTC+2).

**Q: I logged in but my streak broke. Why?**
A: You must log in within 24 hours of your last login. Missing one day breaks the streak.

**Q: Can I fix a broken streak?**
A: No, but your longest streak is saved! Start a new one today.

### Rewards

**Q: When do I get my discount?**
A: Immediately! Discount auto-applies at your current tier.

**Q: Do discounts apply to partner bookings?**
A: Yes, all bookings unless partner opts out (rare).

**Q: Can I use discounts with promo codes?**
A: Usually yes, but some promos exclude tier discounts (check terms).
