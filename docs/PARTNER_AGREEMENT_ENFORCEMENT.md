## 1. PARTNER AGREEMENT TERMS

### 1.1 Master Partner Agreement

Partners must acknowledge and accept ALL of the following terms before onboarding:

#### 1.1.1 Service Level Agreement (SLA)

**Response & Confirmation Times**:
```
REQUIREMENT 1: Response to Guest Inquiries
- Booking inquiries: Must respond within 2 hours
- Measured: From guest message time to partner reply
- Calculation: Average response time tracked daily
- Exceptions: Seasonal hours must be specified in listing

REQUIREMENT 2: Booking Confirmation
- Tentative bookings: Confirm within 4 hours
- Final confirmation: Complete and sign contracts within 24 hours
- Payment deposit: Collect per platform terms
- Late confirmation: Mark guest reservation at risk

REQUIREMENT 3: Guest Communication
- Availability: 24/7 or specify exact hours in listing
- Emergency contact: For guest safety issues
- Check-in instructions: Clear, advance communication
- Issues: Respond to problems within 2 hours

REQUIREMENT 4: Platform Uptime Obligations
- Property data: Keep current and accurate
- Availability calendar: Update within 24 hours
- Photos: Maintain quality standards
- Pricing: No unauthorized price gouging (see 4.2.3)

REQUIREMENT 5: Payment Settlement
- Automatic payouts: 5 business days after guest checkout
- No holds: Only during disputes or chargebacks
- Transparency: Clear payment breakdown itemized
- Reconciliation: Monthly settlement statement
```

**SLA Penalties**:
```
RESPONSE TIME VIOLATIONS:

Yellow Flag (1st offense in 30 days):
- Automated warning via email
- Alert guest of delayed confirmation
- Schedule 1:1 call with partner
- 3-day improvement period

Red Flag (2nd offense in 30 days):
- 24-hour platform suspension
- Prevent new bookings
- Escalate to Governance Team
- Final warning: Cure required immediately

Escalation (3rd offense in 30 days):
- 7-day suspension from platform
- All bookings transferred to support
- Partner must submit improvement plan
- Next violation = permanent termination

Persistent Violation (>3 in 90 days):
- Permanent account suspension
- Property delisting
- Partnership terminated
- Partner blacklist added
```

#### 1.1.2 Quality Standards Agreement

**Property Listing Requirements**:
```
REQUIREMENT 1: Accurate Descriptions
- Clear listing of all amenities (actual, not aspirational)
- Accurate square footage/capacity
- Honest disclosure of limitations (no elevator, no WiFi, etc.)
- Pet policies clearly stated
- Parking information specific

REQUIREMENT 2: Photo & Media Quality
- Minimum 12 high-quality photos required
- All rooms must be photographed
- No filtered/unrealistic images
- Accurate representation of lighting/size
- No stock photos

REQUIREMENT 3: Pricing Transparency
- Base price clearly stated per night
- All mandatory fees listed upfront
- Cleaning fee separate and reasonable
- No hidden charges
- Currency and payment methods clear

REQUIREMENT 4: Cleanliness Standards
- Professional cleaning between guests (MANDATORY)
- Guest reviews: Maintain average ≥3.5 stars
- Monthly inspections for properties <3.5 average
- Failed inspection: 30-day delisting

REQUIREMENT 5: Safety & Compliance
- Working locks on all doors (verified annually)
- Smoke detectors functional
- Emergency contact posted
- House rules clearly visible
- Safety equipment (first aid kit, fire extinguisher)
```

**Quality Penalties**:
```
MISLEADING PHOTOS/DESCRIPTIONS:

First Violation:
- Property delisted immediately
- 30-day review and correction required
- Requires new photos/description approval
- Relistable after correction approval

Second Violation (within 12 months):
- 90-day suspension
- Partner must submit to photography audit
- Relistable after director-level approval only

Third Violation:
- Permanent delisting
- Partnership terminated
- Partner blacklist entry

QUALITY RATINGS:

Rating 3.5-5.0:
- All systems normal
- Continue operations

Rating 3.0-3.4:
- Yellow flag: Improvement plan required
- Mandatory 30-day action plan submission
- Focus on specific complaints

Rating <3.0:
- Red flag: 30-day probation
- Listing may be delisted if not improved
- Weekly check-ins required
- Third inspection failure = delisting
```

#### 1.1.3 Cancellation & Refund Policy

**Partner Obligations**:
```
REQUIREMENT 1: Honor Reservations
- Partner MUST honor all confirmed bookings
- Cannot cancel without legitimate cause
- Legitimate causes:
  ✓ Guest violation of house rules (verified)
  ✓ Uncontrollable circumstances (fire, flood, death in family)
  ✓ Government mandate (lockdown, evacuation order)
  
REQUIREMENT 2: Refund Processing
- Refunds processed within 7 business days
- Amount: 100% of guest payment (no deductions)
- Direct deposit to original payment method
- No "processing fees" or deductions

REQUIREMENT 3: Cancellation Communication
- If cancellation necessary, notify immediately
- Provide 24-hour notice minimum (or rescind cancellation)
- Assist guest in finding alternative property
- Offer partner's alternative dates at same rate
```

**Cancellation Penalties**:
```
ARBITRARY CANCELLATION PATTERN:

Single Cancellation:
- Automatic refund + platform credit to guest
- Partner receives warning notice
- Report filed for monitoring

Two Cancellations in 30 Days:
- Automatic probation (30 days)
- All new bookings require pre-approval
- Guest compensation: 20% of booking value
- Failure to improve = suspension

Pattern Identified (>2 in 30 days):
- 7-day suspension
- Cancellations blocked automatically
- Must appeal to Governance Team
- Next pattern = permanent termination

Persistent Abuse (>40% cancellation rate):
- Immediate delisting
- Partnership terminated
- Blacklist added
```

#### 1.1.4 Data Security & Payment Compliance

**Non-Negotiable Requirements**:
```
REQUIREMENT 1: Payment Card Security
❌ NEVER collect guest payment cards
❌ NEVER store card information
❌ NEVER request CVV or PIN
✅ ONLY use platform payment processing
✅ ONLY accept platform payment methods

REQUIREMENT 2: Data Protection
✅ Comply with POPI Act (South Africa)
✅ Protect guest contact information
✅ Do not share data with third parties
✅ Do not use guest data for marketing (without consent)
❌ NEVER sell or lease guest data
❌ NEVER use data outside of guest services

REQUIREMENT 3: Communication
✅ Use platform messaging exclusively
✅ Respond to guest inquiries
❌ NEVER request payment outside platform
❌ NEVER exchange personal details for booking

REQUIREMENT 4: Record Retention
- Keep guest information only while needed
- Delete data within 30 days of checkout
- Comply with POPI Act data retention rules
- No indefinite data collection
```

**Data Security Violations** (IMMEDIATE TERMINATION):
```
Automatic Suspension/Termination:

Card Data Collection:
- Immediate account suspension
- Investigation: 48 hours
- Termination recommended (unless isolated error)
- Law enforcement referral if fraud detected

Data Sharing Without Consent:
- Account suspended pending investigation
- 72-hour investigation period
- Termination if breach confirmed
- POPI Act regulator notification

Payment Solicitation Outside Platform:
- Account suspended
- Guest investigation
- If confirmed: Termination + refund to all guests
- Fraud team investigation required
```

#### 1.1.5 Non-Discrimination Policy

**Mandatory Compliance**:
```
REQUIREMENT 1: Equal Treatment
✅ Accept all guests equally
✅ No discrimination based on:
   - Race, color, ethnic origin
   - Gender, gender identity, sexual orientation
   - Religion or belief
   - Age, disability, veteran status
   - Family status, marital status
   - National origin, citizenship
   - Language

REQUIREMENT 2: Fair Cancellation Practices
- Cannot cancel for protected characteristics
- Cannot offer inferior service based on demographics
- Cannot exclude based on accent, language, or appearance
- Must treat all guests per posted house rules (equally)

REQUIREMENT 3: Accessibility
- Provide accurate accessibility information
- Service animals permitted (legal requirement)
- Reasonable accommodations for disabilities
- Accessible payment/communication options
```

**Discrimination Violations** (IMMEDIATE TERMINATION - NO APPEAL):
```
Any substantiated discrimination complaint results in:

1. Immediate account suspension
2. All bookings transferred to support
3. Guest compensation (20-100% refund + $500 credit)
4. 72-hour investigation
5. Termination decision
6. Permanent blacklist entry
7. Law enforcement referral
8. Public statement on anti-discrimination stance

NO SECOND CHANCES - Zero tolerance policy
Permanent ban from CollEco Travel ecosystem
Shared with partner networks (blacklist)
```

### 2.2 Escalation Matrix

**Tier-Based Response System**:

```
TIER 1: YELLOW FLAG (Monitoring)
├─ Trigger: Minor metric deviation
├─ Examples:
│  - Response time: 2-3 hours average
│  - Cancellation: 1 in 30 days
│  - Rating: 3.5-4.0 average
│  - Complaint: Single quality issue
├─ Action:
│  - Automated email alert to partner
│  - Alert guest that confirmation may be delayed
│  - No booking restrictions
│  - 3-day improvement monitoring
└─ Escalation: Continue monitoring, escalate if trend continues

TIER 2: RED FLAG (Action Required)
├─ Trigger: Significant metric deviation
├─ Examples:
│  - Response time: >3 hours average
│  - Cancellation: 2 in 30 days
│  - Rating: <3.5 average
│  - Multiple complaints in 7 days
├─ Action:
│  - Phone call from Partner Relations team
│  - Mandatory improvement plan submission (48 hours)
│  - Temporary booking restrictions while plan reviewed
│  - 7-day monitoring period
└─ Escalation: Automatic if not improved, escalate to TIER 3

TIER 3: ESCALATION (Governance Review)
├─ Trigger: Sustained issues, unresponsive to improvement plan
├─ Examples:
│  - Continued yellow/red flags after improvement plan
│  - 3rd violation in 30 days
│  - Guest safety incident
│  - Fraud indication
├─ Action:
│  - Governance Team investigation (48 hours)
│  - Partner interview/explanation opportunity
│  - Temporary 24-72 hour suspension
│  - Final determination: Cure, probation, or termination
└─ Escalation: To TIER 4 if violation confirmed

TIER 4: ENFORCEMENT (Suspension or Termination)
├─ Trigger: Serious violations, policy breaches, safety risks
├─ Examples:
│  - Persistent SLA violations
│  - Data/payment security breaches
│  - Discrimination complaint substantiated
│  - Fraud detected
│  - >3 serious violations in 6 months
├─ Action:
│  - Immediate property delisting
│  - Partnership suspension (7-30 days) or termination
│  - All bookings transferred to support
│  - Guest refunds and compensation
│  - Law enforcement referral (if applicable)
│  - Blacklist entry for termination cases
└─ Escalation: Legal and regulatory reporting
```

### 2.3 Appeal Process

**Partner Rights & Recourse**:

```
SUSPENSION/TERMINATION APPEALS:

Step 1: Appeal Submission (Within 30 days of decision)
- Written appeal with supporting documentation
- Explanation of why decision was incorrect
- Evidence of improvement measures taken
- Request for review by different team

Step 2: Appeal Review (15 business days)
- Different staff member reviews (not original decision maker)
- Partner given opportunity to present case
- Interview or video call if requested
- Evidence is considered and evaluated
- Site inspection conducted if relevant

Step 3: Final Decision (5 business days after review)
- Appeal sustained: Decision reversed, full account restoration
- Appeal denied: Original decision upheld, no further appeals
- Partial remedy: Compromise solution (e.g., probation instead of ban)
- Written decision with detailed reasoning

Step 4: Escalation (If needed)
- Can request CEO/Board level review
- Board review within 30 days
- Executive review decision is FINAL
- No further appeals permitted

TYPES ELIGIBLE FOR APPEAL:
✅ Temporary suspensions
✅ Quality probation
✅ SLA violation penalties
✅ Payment holds
✅ Wrongful fraud allegations

TYPES NOT ELIGIBLE FOR APPEAL:
❌ Safety violations (guest endangerment)
❌ Discrimination findings
❌ Payment card theft/fraud
❌ Data breach (deliberate)
❌ Terrorism/extremism
❌ Child exploitation
```

## 3. PARTNER TERMINATION FRAMEWORK

### 3.1 Immediate Termination (No Cure Period)

**Zero-Tolerance Violations**:

```
CATEGORY 1: GUEST SAFETY ENDANGERMENT
Triggers:
- Unsafe living conditions discovered (no locks, structural hazards)
- Guest reported serious health/safety incident
- Property condemned by authorities
- History of violence/threats at property
- Illegal activity (drugs, weapons, stolen goods)

Action:
- Immediate delisting (within 1 hour)
- Account suspended immediately
- All active bookings cancelled + full refund
- Alternative accommodation provided to guests
- Law enforcement notified if safety crime

CATEGORY 2: FRAUD & FINANCIAL CRIME
Triggers:
- Stealing/collecting payment outside platform
- Card data collection
- Chargeback fraud pattern
- Creating fake accounts for artificial reviews
- Money laundering activity

Action:
- Account frozen immediately
- Funds held pending investigation (30 days)
- Full guest refunds from held funds
- Law enforcement report filed
- Financial institution notification
- Permanent blacklist entry

CATEGORY 3: DISCRIMINATION & HATE CRIMES
Triggers:
- Substantiated discrimination complaint
- Racial, gender, or sexual orientation slurs
- Refusal of service based on protected class
- Harassment of guests or staff

Action:
- Immediate account termination (NO APPEAL)
- All bookings cancelled
- 100% refund to all affected guests
- $500 platform credit per affected guest
- Law enforcement report
- Public statement on discrimination policy
- Permanent blacklist

CATEGORY 4: DATA PROTECTION VIOLATIONS (Deliberate)
Triggers:
- Selling guest data
- Sharing data with third parties for profit
- Collecting data beyond service provision
- POPI Act compliance violation (intentional)

Action:
- Immediate account suspension
- 48-hour investigation
- Law enforcement notification
- POPI Act regulator notification
- Termination decision
- Guest compensation
- Permanent blacklist

CATEGORY 5: CHILD SAFETY VIOLATIONS
Triggers:
- Any indication of child exploitation
- Grooming or inappropriate behavior
- Sexual content involving minors
- Failure to report suspicion to authorities

Action:
- Immediate account termination
- Law enforcement report filed IMMEDIATELY
- NCMEC (or local equivalent) notification
- All records preserved for investigation
- Cooperation with authorities
- Permanent global blacklist
```

### 3.2 Suspension with Cure Period

**Violations with Opportunity to Improve**:

```
SLA VIOLATIONS (Repeated)
First-Time: Warning
Second (within 30 days): 24-hour suspension
Third (within 30 days): 7-day suspension + improvement plan
Fourth (within 30 days): Termination

QUALITY ISSUES (Maintenance)
Rating 3.0-3.4: 30-day improvement plan required
- Photo quality audit
- Guest complaint analysis
- Improvement metrics tracked
- 30-day extension possible if showing progress
- Delisting if <3.0 maintained

CANCELLATION PATTERNS
>1 cancellation/month: Yellow flag
>2 cancellations in 30 days: Red flag + probation
>3 in 30 days: 7-day suspension
>40% cancellation rate: Delisting + termination

PAYMENT DISPUTES
Chargeback filed: Funds held pending investigation
Chargeback substantiated: Partner liable for refund
Pattern of chargebacks: Account review + possible restrictions
Multiple unresolved disputes: Suspension pending resolution
```

### 3.3 Blacklist Management

**Partner Blacklist Categories**:

```
PERMANENT BLACKLIST (No Re-entry):
- Discrimination violations (substantiated)
- Child safety violations
- Payment card fraud
- Deliberate data protection breach
- Multiple terminations in 12 months
- Violence/threats toward guests
- Terrorism/extremism activity

Consequences:
- Cannot open new account
- Cannot partner with CollEco Travel
- Information shared with partner networks
- Background check includes blacklist

TEMPORARY BLACKLIST (1-3 Years):
- Termination for SLA violations
- Termination for quality violations
- Fraud (non-card related)
- Multiple suspensions

Consequences:
- Cannot reapply until period ends
- Can appeal for early removal after 6 months
- Limited to higher-touch review process

RE-ENTRY PROCESS (After Temporary Blacklist):
- New application required
- Background investigation
- Character references
- Site inspection
- Trial period: 3 months with daily monitoring
- Good standing required for 6 months
- Probation: First 12 months on platform
```

## 5. DISPUTE RESOLUTION & CONFLICT MANAGEMENT

### 5.1 Guest-Partner Disputes

**Process**:
```
Step 1: Direct Communication (24 hours)
- Guest and partner have 24 hours to resolve
- Platform provides mediation templates
- Focus on mutually acceptable solution

Step 2: Platform Mediation (48 hours)
- If unresolved, platform reviews situation
- Proposes mediation solution (50/50 compromise)
- Both parties respond within 48 hours

Step 3: Platform Determination (72 hours)
- Platform decision maker reviews all evidence
- Determines liability and resolution
- 72 hours for decision

Step 4: Resolution Implementation
- Approved party receives compensation
- Opposite party notified
- Records filed for monitoring
- Pattern monitoring activated if applicable

Common Resolutions:
- Partial refund (25-75% of booking value)
- Full refund
- Free future booking credit
- Combination of above
```

**Guest Compensation Authority**:
```
Guest Receives Compensation For:
- Cancellation by partner (100% refund)
- Misrepresented property (50-100% refund)
- Safety/cleanliness issues (50-100% refund)
- Lack of amenities (25-50% refund)
- Communication failure (10-25% refund)
- Discrimination (100% refund + $500 credit)

Partner Liable For:
- Compensating guest from their account
- Non-payment results in account suspension
- Pattern of compensation = escalation to enforcement
```
